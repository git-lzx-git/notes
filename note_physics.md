# 弹性碰撞
$$m_1v_1+m_2v_2=m_1v_1^\prime+m_2v_2^\prime$$

$$\frac12m_1v_1^2+\frac12m_2v_2^2=\frac12m_1v_2^{\prime2}+\frac12m_2v_2^{\prime2}$$

$$v_1^\prime=\frac{(m_1-m_2)v_1+2m_2v_2}{m_1+m_2}$$

$$v_2^\prime=\frac{(m_2-m_1)v_2+2m_1v_1}{m_1+m_2}$$
